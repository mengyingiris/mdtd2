VIRTUAL GALLERY LAUNCH

DECEMBER 10th 6PM CT
Part One (June ‘21) A 12-hour showcase of student artist work streamed consecutively online, as well as projected live at physical locations in the Chicago area.
Part Two (December ‘21) The Time-Based Virtual Art Experience. Presented and Hosted on the New Art City platform.
Part Two of My Dreams These Days is a student-led re-conceptualization of what it means to present in a gallery space. Produced and curated by students for students, the gallery is focused on time based work, including but not limited to: sound, film, new media, performance, art and technology, time-based sculpture, etc.
Following the themes of part one, the gallery will consist of three curated stages with a focus on the following conceptual prompts: Material, Virtual and Rhythmic. The goal of this student artist collective is to facilitate a space for sharing creative visions in a post-pandemic context, while opening the possibility for an ongoing online archive for community building, collaboration and discussion. This virtual gallery is being generously hosted by New Art City, a virtual gallery building platform working with educational institutions across the country to help build the new world of virtual experiences.
